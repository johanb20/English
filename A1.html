  • Present perfect yes/no and Wh-questions, statements, and short answers with regular and irregular past participles → Presente perfecto: preguntas de sí/no y Wh, oraciones afirmativas y respuestas cortas con participios pasados regulares e irregulares
  • Already and yet → Uso de already (ya) y yet (todavía / aún)
  • Present perfect vs. simple past → Diferencia entre presente perfecto y pasado simple
  • For and since → Uso de for (durante) y since (desde)

Present Perfect; Already and Yet
The present perfect is formed with the verb have + the past participle.
Question Answer

No, he hasn't visited any parks.

No, they haven't eaten yet.

Present perfect vs. simple past
Use the present perfect for an indefinite time in the past.
Question Answer
